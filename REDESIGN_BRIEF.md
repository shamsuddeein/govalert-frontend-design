## PART 1 — WHAT'S BROKEN (Filter List)

These are the exact problems to fix before anything else. No redesign should carry these forward.

### Critical Kills
1. **Logo is a placeholder.** A dark square with a "G" is not a brand. It communicates nothing.
2. **Color palette is a Tailwind default.** Navy `#0f172a` + bright green `#22c55e` = every AI-generated SaaS template from 2023. There is zero brand personality.
3. **All job cards show "12 openings."** This is clearly unseeded dummy data that was never replaced. On a trust platform, this destroys credibility instantly.
4. **Inconsistency between light and dark mode job cards.** In light mode: colored left border by status. In dark mode: border disappears entirely. The system is broken.
